Jundiaí - SP - Brasil is a very good municipality in the countryside of the state of Sao paulo recognised for high quality of life,strong industrial economy, listed among ther best cities in Brasil. It has several cool places to kill some time with your family like Citu Park, Children's World, Botanic Garden an the wine district, with several wine. The traditional Grape Festival is a cultural celebration of the city

Interesting facts and historical information about Jundiaí is The name is a reference to the Jundiaí River, whose name comes from the Tupi language and means "River of the Jundiás", a type of fish that was then characteristic of the region.
